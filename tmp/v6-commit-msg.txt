fix(roadmap-hero): drop textLength/lengthAdjust, widen plaques, restore natural rendering

v5 used textLength + lengthAdjust="spacingAndGlyphs" to defeat
GitHub's Times-Roman fallback cut-off, but on Chrome with Georgia
available this stretched every glyph ("M V P v 0 . 1", "Q U A T E R N I U S  L I V E").

v6 strategy:
- Drop ALL textLength + lengthAdjust attributes (let glyphs render naturally)
- Widen <rect> plaques ~25% (longest natural text × 1.25)
  so Times Roman fallback still fits without clipping
- explicit font-family Georgia,'Times New Roman',serif on all 32 text elements
- modest letter-spacing (0.6 captions, 1.4 status pills) — never on constrained text
- status pills shift to translate(0 -88), title plaques to translate(0 -52)
- eyebrow ornament glyphs ❖ retained (eyebrow only, not subtitle headers)

Anti-stretch markers: textLength=0, lengthAdjust=0 ✓
